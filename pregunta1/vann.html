Mandor Vann

  • Uncle of Isia and Juliana Vann.
  • Served in the Kronos military at different embassies.
  • Emerged as the group's top political strategist.
  • Intense, intelligent, organized and ambitious.
  • POK members have great respect for him.

Vann spent much of his military career serving at the Kronosian embassies in Tethys and Asteria.
