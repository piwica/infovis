Carmine Osvaldo

  • Served in the Kronosian military with Bodrogi.
  • Member of the Board of Elodis Chamber of Commerce.
  • Because of his business experience, Osvaldo became the primary navigator of the relationship between the burgeoning social movement, the Kronos government and outside agencies.
  • Osvaldo proposed to the activists to form a social movement organization with an identity brand and a specific agenda: Protectors of Kronos (POK)

Osvaldo is a longlife friend of Bodrogi. He contacted Wellness for All (WFA), an international agency that confirmed contamination in the waters of Tiskele River. After Bodrogi stepped down as leader of POK, Osvaldo followed him.
