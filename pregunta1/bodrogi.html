Henk Bodrogi

  • Experience with floodplain agriculture techniques.
  • One of the seven founding members of the Protectors of Kronos.
  • Well-respected member of the community, involved with civic business groups and considered a natural leader.
  • During the early stages of the group, Henk Bodrogi emerged as the leader.
  • Served in the Kronosian military.
  • Lacked experience in dealing with the government.
  • In 2001, Bodrogi stepped down as leader of the POK, formally handing his responsibilities to Elian Karel.

Bodrogi was an initial actor in the group after his wife became ill with symptoms of cardiopulmonary disease, consistent with ethylene glycol contamination.

Before his departure of the group, he also began to suffer health problems associated to contamination.
